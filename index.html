About Me

AJ Javadi is currently a student at Northwestern's Full Time Coding bootcamp. He has a background in Healthcare Consulting and Data Analysis. After receiving his Bachelor's of Arts from the Univeristy of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in Political Science and Informatics, he has worked for several major players in the Healthcare Industry, such as Cerner and Guidehouse, where he served as an Implementation Consultant and Data Analyst, respectfully. Outside of work, his passion projects include Music Production, DJing, and Open Source Software. His main goal is to combine his passion for Technology and Music Production to create applications that can help up and coming artists grow their fanbase, and connect fans to upcoming artists near them. Stay tuned for more in 2023!
